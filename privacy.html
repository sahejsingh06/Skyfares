Data Protection

Privacy Policy.

Commitment to handling your data with full confidentiality and professional discretion.

1 Information Collection

We only collect the minimum information required to design your flight strategy and facilitate bookings:

  • Identity Information: Full name and passport details (required by airlines for international travel).
  • Contact Information: Email address and phone number for communication throughout the booking process.
  • Travel Preferences: Desired routes, travel dates, cabin class preferences, and loyalty program balances.

2 How We Use Your Data

Your information is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Creating bespoke flight itineraries and redemption strategies.
  • Communicating with airlines to verify seat availability and secure bookings.
  • Providing updates on your flight status and delivering your PNR/E-ticket.
  • Internal record-keeping for service auditing.

3 Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. All sensitive data is handled via secure communication channels and stored in protected environments.

4 Sharing of Information

Skyfare Consulting has a strict zero-sale policy for client data. Your information is only shared with:

  • Airlines: To facilitate the booking and ticketing process.
  • Service Partners: Vetted third-party providers required for specific logistics (e.g., visa assistance or airport transfers) where explicitly requested by you.

5 Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy, or to comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

6 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request the deletion of your data once the service is complete (subject to legal requirements).

7 Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out to us at info@skyfareconsulting.com or via our WhatsApp support line.
