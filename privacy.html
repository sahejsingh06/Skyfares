Privacy Policy

At Skyfare Consulting, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as our client.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use our services:

  • Personal details: Name, email, phone number, and country of residence.
  • Travel preferences: Routes, dates, cabin class, loyalty programs, and points balances (only if you provide them).
  • Technical data: Basic usage data when you visit our website (cookies, browser type, approximate location).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for:

  • Designing flight strategies and itineraries tailored to your needs.
  • Advising on loyalty program usage, award bookings, and cash vs. miles comparisons.
  • Communicating with you about options, updates, and follow-up support.
  • Improving our services and website experience.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or trade your personal data. We only share your information with:

  • Trusted partners (for optional concierge or booking support, if you request it).
  • Service providers (e.g., calendar scheduling tools like Calendly, or payment processors if applicable).
  • When required by law or legal process.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no system is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Ask for corrections or updates to inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your data (“right to be forgotten”).
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

7. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies for basic analytics and functionality. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function properly.

8. Third-Party Links

Our site may link to airline websites, loyalty programs, or booking tools. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties, and we encourage you to review their policies.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page with a “last updated” date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us.
