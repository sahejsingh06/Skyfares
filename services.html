Frequently Asked Questions

Is nominee redemption allowed?
Yes—as per airline program rules, members can nominate individuals for award tickets. We facilitate the entire search, strategy, and booking process to ensure compliance and best results.
What taxes are included?
Fees quoted by us are for the points/redemption service only. Government-mandated airport taxes and airline fuel surcharges are charged separately at the time of ticketing.
How fast can we ticket?
Once confirmed award space is found and verified, ticketing can often be completed within the same business day, especially for nominee redemptions.
