Case Studies

A couple of realistic, representative wins. Find how we strategize flights for our clients.

We combine award charts, transfer bonuses, legal stopovers, and cash/miles mixing to get premium value.

SIN → JFK (Business), single traveller

Wanted Business Class on specific dates without paying peak cash fares.

  • Strategy: Timed a bank-points transfer bonus to a partner with a South-Asia ↔ North America sweet spot; booked partner award and used a short positioning hop to the hub.
  • Outlay: 111,000 miles + ~$160–$220 in taxes/fees (typical range).
  • Cash compare: Same dates priced around $4,500 for Business Class round-trip.
  • Estimated savings: ~55–70% vs cash, depending on how you value the miles and the transfer bonus.

Why it worked: transfer bonus timing + favorable partner pricing + low surcharges via the chosen program.

LAX → LHR (Family of 3), school-holiday window

Parents + child with fixed outbound date during peak season.

  • Strategy: Mixed itinerary—cash outbound on a lower-surcharge carrier into LGW; return booked with Avios on an off-peak band (booked when calendar opened). Seats selected together.
  • Outlay: ~$1,150 total outbound cash + ~75,000 Avios return + ~$220 taxes/fees.
  • Cash compare: Fully-cash round-trip for 3 on those dates was ~$2,900–$3,300.
  • Estimated savings: ~30–40% vs cash while keeping family-friendly times and same-day connections.

Why it worked: split cash/award, early booking of off-peak dates, and routing into an airport with lower fees.

*Illustrative examples only; programs and charts change. We design the best play for your dates, miles, and tolerance for positioning.
