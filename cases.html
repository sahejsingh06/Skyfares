Case Studies

A couple of realistic, representative wins. These are illustrative examples; availability and pricing vary.

DEL → JFK (Business), single traveller

Needed round-trip Business at short notice. Cash fares were high.

  • Strategy: Used bank-points transfer promo (+30%) to a partner program; booked a Star Alliance partner award with a legal stopover. Short positioning hop included to reach the partner’s hub.
  • Outlay: ~95–110k miles round-trip + ₹20–25k taxes/fees.
  • Cash compare: same dates priced ~₹4.8–5.4L cash.
  • Estimated savings: ~60–70% vs. cash (value of miles + transfer bonus).

Why it worked: transfer bonus timing, partner sweet spot between South Asia ↔ North America, and lower surcharges via the chosen program.

BOM → LON (Family of 3), school-holiday dates

Parents + child, fixed outbound date constraints during peak.

  • Strategy: Mixed itinerary—cash outbound on a lower-surcharge carrier into LGW, Avios return on off-peak dates using Reward Flight Saver; seats held as soon as calendar opened.
  • Outlay: ~₹85–95k cash outbound total + ~60–70k Avios return + ~₹18–22k taxes/fees.
  • Cash compare: fully-cash round-trip quotes were ~₹2.1–2.4L.
  • Estimated savings: ~35–45% while keeping family-friendly times and same-day connections.

Why it worked: split cash/award, off-peak calendar targeting, and routing into an airport with lower fees.

*All figures are indicative; programs, charts and surcharges change. We design the best play given your dates, miles and tolerance for positioning.
