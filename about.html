Why most people miss out on premium seats

Most travelers want to fly comfortably, but miles programs are confusing. Partners, charts, transfer bonuses, surcharges, married segments—there’s a lot. As a result, miles sit idle, get used on poor-value redemptions, or expire.

🤯

Complex rules

Partner availability, zone charts, and peak calendars change constantly.

💸

Value leaks

Many redemptions return only 0.7–1.0¢ per point; sweet spots can unlock 2–6¢+.

⏱️

Timing matters

Award space opens and disappears fast. Transfer timing can make or break a booking.

Skyfare exists to bridge this gap. We track programs daily, spot partner availability across alliances, and stack transfer bonuses and stopover rules to move you into premium cabins—often for a fraction of cash fares.
