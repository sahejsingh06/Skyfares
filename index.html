Frequently Requested

Quick answers to common enquiries. For more details, visit our full FAQ page.

What if there’s no award space on my dates?
We propose 1–2 alternates (date/route) and can use nominee redemption paths that avoid waitlists. Our experts are skilled at finding hidden availability that doesn't always show on standard search engines.
Do I need to add you as a nominee?
Only if you choose nominee redemption; we’ll share simple, step-by-step instructions. Direct transfer works, too. We ensure the process is transparent and handled with the utmost security.
How fast can I fly?
Nominee bookings can be immediate once space is secured. Direct transfers typically complete in ≈5 business days. For urgent travel, we recommend contacting us immediately via WhatsApp.
