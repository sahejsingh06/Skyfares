3 Ticket Ownership

Once a ticket has been issued, the ticket belongs entirely to the passenger listed on the booking. The airline manages all future changes, upgrades, and requests relating to the ticket.

Passengers will receive the booking reference (PNR) and may manage their booking directly with the airline.

4 Changes to Booking

Passengers may request changes to the date or destination of their flight if the airline allows modifications.

Changes are only possible if the required miles for the alternative flight are the same or lower than the original booking.

Passengers must contact the airline directly to request such changes. This can usually be done through the airline’s live chat service or customer service hotline.

Skyfare Consulting cannot guarantee that the airline will approve any changes.

5 Cancellation Policy

If a passenger wishes to cancel or modify their travel plans, they may contact the airline directly to explore options such as changing the travel date or destination.

Passengers may request to use the ticket for another date or holiday in the future by contacting the airline through their live chat service or customer support.
