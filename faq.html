Common Questions, Expert Answers.

Everything you need to know about our flight strategies, miles redemptions, and how we deliver exceptional travel value.

Are you a travel agency?
No. We’re a flight strategy consultancy. We design your itinerary and show you how to book (or connect you to partners for concierge ticketing). Our focus is on maximizing your value, not selling tickets.
How do your fees work?
We believe in transparent pricing. Assessment Calls (S$99) determine feasibility, Itinerary Design (from S$240) handles the deep strategy, and our nominee redemptions follow a clear tiered rate. No hidden mark-ups.
Can you guarantee availability?
While no one can guarantee airline inventory, we use sophisticated monitoring tools and program arbitrage to significantly increase your odds compared to standard searches.
Is nominee redemption legal/allowed?
Yes. KrisFlyer's programme rules allow members to redeem miles for nominated individuals. We operate fully within the programme's terms — we simply handle the search, strategy, and booking process on your behalf.
Buying miles vs. Skyfare Strategy?
Buying miles directly from Singapore Airlines is significantly more expensive. We source miles at a fraction of the retail price, then redeem them for confirmed Business or First Class seats — giving you premium travel at a fraction of the cash fare.
Is my personal data safe?
Absolutely. We only collect the minimum information needed to make your booking — name, passport details, and travel dates. Your data is never shared with third parties and is handled with full confidentiality.
What airlines can you book for me?
We work across a wide range of airlines including Singapore Airlines, ANA, Air Canada, Lufthansa, Swiss, Air India, Sri Lankan Airlines, Cathay Pacific, Qatar Airways, British Airways, JAL, Royal Air Maroc, and more.
Do I need to be a KrisFlyer member?
Not necessarily. For nominee redemptions, you don't need your own KrisFlyer account — we handle the redemption on your behalf. For direct transfers, a KrisFlyer account in your name is required.
Do you work with corporate clients?
Yes. We handle multi-traveler bookings, complex route playbooks for executives, and audit existing corporate reward programs to ensure zero waste.
What about refunds and changes?
Redemptions following our strategies typically benefit from the airline's standard award policies, which often offer better flexibility than restrictive discount cash fares. We'll outline these before you book.
How fast do I get my itinerary?
Standard route strategies are delivered within 24-48 hours of your Assessment Call. Multi-city global tours may take up to 4 business days for comprehensive modeling.
About the Nov 2025 KrisFlyer Update
We have updated all our redemption slabs to reflect the Singapore Airlines KrisFlyer changes effective 1 Nov 2025. This ensures your quotes are always based on the latest available inventory and mileage requirements.
What is the "Available Nominee" redemption?
This is our premier service where we use our established network of miles holders to ticket your flight as a nominee. It is often faster than direct transfers and provides access to confirmed seats without the waitlist hassle.
How long does the process take?
Nominee redemptions can be ticketed the same business day once seats are confirmed and payment is received. Direct transfers typically take 3–4 weeks. We'll always give you a clear timeline upfront.
What if there's no availability?
We'll propose 1–2 alternative dates or routings that work for your trip. Our job is to find the best available option — we monitor award space actively and know which routes open up and when.
Can you book for my family/group?
Yes. We regularly handle bookings for couples, families, and small groups. Miles requirements and pricing will be confirmed based on the number of passengers and cabin class.
