FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions about how we plan routes, use miles, and compare cash fares.

Strategy-first Miles & cash mix Transparent pricing
Are you a travel agency? +

No. We are a flight strategy consultancy. We don’t issue tickets. We design your itinerary and provide booking guidance (and optional concierge through partners).

How do your fees work? +

Simple and transparent: a fixed Assessment for quick feasibility, Itinerary Design for deep work, and Miles Concierge if you want ongoing monitoring/alerts. No mark-ups on tickets.

Can you guarantee availability? +

We can’t guarantee award or fare availability. We improve odds by timing searches, using partner programs, and advising on transfer and waitlist strategies.

Do you work with corporate clients? +

Yes. We build route playbooks, handle complex team windows, align with policy, and leverage corporate reward programs.

Will you use my miles or cash—or both? +

We model both. Often the best value is a mix: cash one way on a low-surcharge carrier, miles on the return, timed with transfer bonuses.

How long does it take to get options? +

After the Assessment call, we typically share 1–2 high-value options within 24–48 hours for most trips (complex multi-city may take a bit longer).

Refunds & changes—how do they work? +

Policies depend on each airline/program. We’ll flag change/refund rules, re-deposit fees, and fare conditions so you know the trade-offs before booking.

Are there any hidden fees? +

No. Our pricing is transparent. Taxes/airline surcharges are paid directly to the airline or program—we’ll estimate them up front.

How do you handle my personal data? +

We collect only what’s needed to design your trip (dates, routes, preferences). See our Privacy Policy for details.

Still have a question?

We’ll look at your dates, routes, and miles and outline the best play.
