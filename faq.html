FAQ

Are you a travel agency?+

No. We are a flight strategy consultancy. We don’t issue tickets. We design your itinerary and provide booking guidance (and optional concierge through partners).

Can you guarantee availability?+

We can’t guarantee award or fare availability, but we search the best fares for you, leverage partners, and advise on transfer and waitlist strategies to maximize success.

Do you work with corporate clients?+

Yes. We design repeatable route playbooks, handle complex team travel windows, and align with corporate policies and reward programs.
